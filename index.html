Works in: Chrome and Edge on Windows, Safari on iPhone (iOS 14.5 or later, Siri & Dictation must be enabled in Settings). Recognition runs through your browser's speech engine, so an internet connection is required. Filler sounds are removed and capitalisation is applied automatically. On a keyboard, Ctrl + Space starts and stops dictation.

If the microphone button is blocked (some embedded viewers never allow it): tap into the transcript box and dictate with your system's own microphone instead: the mic key on the iPhone keyboard, or Win + H on Windows. Then tap Clean up to apply the same filler removal, punctuation commands, and capitalisation to that text.
